ELVIA

Champion Triathlete

Elvia has been a software Lecturer at Temasek Polytechnic, is a MetaSport triathlon coach and is currently studying to be a nutritionist.

Elvia has been triathlon training for nine years and in that time has participated in numerous triathlons across South East Asia coming first amongst all women in the 2013 Mega Triathlon, Third amongst all women in the 2013 Bintan Triathlon and second and third in the women’s 35-39 age-group category in the 2014 and 2015 IronMan 70.3 Putrajaya.

Elvia explains ‘‘When I started triathlon training I didn’t know how to ride a bike, I’d never even owned a bike, so I had to really work at it until I turned my weakness into my strength.

I don't think I have a natural talent in any of the 3 disciplines, but my true strength is my determination - I am stubborn enough not to give up on the things I really want to achieve, in anything in life.’’

ELVIA’S REVIEW

Wireless, waterproof

and surprisingly lightweight, with all the latest functions necessary for training.

This is a wireless, waterproof headphone which I can load music into, and it comes with Bluetooth® and all the latest functions necessary for runners (e.g. accelerometer, GPS, heart rate, cadence). It’s an exciting gadget to use for a techie, sporty person like me. I use it during my bike training sessions, runs and swims. It is surprisingly lightweight and stays in place because it fits my ears well.

No more worries! I can work up a sweat with
Sony XperiaTM Z5 as it is

tough and waterproof.

The water-resistant feature is a big plus for me. Sometimes I like to do some reading on the phone while warming up on the bike trainer — especially when I’m doing a long endurance set with a long warm up in the beginning. The Xperia™ Z5 comes in handy because I don't need to worry about sweat dripping or wiping off from my hands onto the phone. The big screen also makes reading on it more enjoyable.

Wireless with great connectivity to

track my daily activities,

heart rate and calories burnt.

Personally, I use this as a wake-up alarm and also to monitor my morning heart rate. The vibrating alarm is pretty cool and wakes me up during my lightweight sleep cycles — I’m getting pretty addicted to it. The band itself is very light and waterproof, comes with Bluetooth® and NFC™ connectivity (just tap to easily connect to my phone) and can be used to track your daily activities (e.g. steps taken, heart rate changes, calories burnt).
