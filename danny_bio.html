DANNY

Singapore National Swimmer

Danny is an eight-time SEA Games Gold medalist, having represented Singapore in the 100m, 200m, 4x100m and 4x200m freestyle events. He currently holds the Singapore national record for the 400m freestyle and swims up to 60km every week to maintain his competitive edge.

The SMU undergraduate was inspired to swim from a young age by his late father, and he counts local swimming legend Ang Peng Siong as a mentor and close family friend.
