WAI MUN

Competitive Cyclist

Wai Mun hasn’t just mastered one sport, but two. He was previously a National Champion rower and represented Singapore at the Asian Games and Asian Rowing Championships before developing his passion for cycling.

In the three years he has been cycling, he has competed twice in the Asian Cycling Championships as part of the National Track Cycling Squad.

As Wai Mun says, ‘You have to be in it to win it, and the first step is the hardest. Getting up every morning to train and just taking part is half the battle won.’’
